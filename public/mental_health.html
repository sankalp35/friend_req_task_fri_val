If you are concerned about your mental health, here are some contacts that you might find useful (please copy and paste the links into a NEW browser window):


Resource Contact
Your GP: Book an appointment with your GP. They can offer advice or refer you to other more specific services to get help.

 Your GP's number
Mind provides information, guidance, and links to support services for those with living with a mental health problem or supporting someone who is.

 Visit: https://www.mind.org.uk/
FRANK provides friendly, confidential drugs advice.

 Visit: http://www.talktofrank.com
or call 0300 123 6600
NHS Choices: Enter your location to discover mental health support services near you.

 Visit: https://www.nhs.uk/Service-Search/Mental%20health%20support/LocationSearch/330
IN AN EMERGENCY: If you are experiencing suicidal thoughts and think that you might be unable to keep yourself safe, visit your nearest A&E department or call emergency services.

 Call: 999
